How To Play¶

Each month’s scenarios can be played with either Classic BattleTech or Alpha Strike. Each scenario will specify the characters to be used and provide scoring for objectives. Your score at the end of the 15 scenarios will determine how closely your play through matches the lore of Mercenary’s Pride.
